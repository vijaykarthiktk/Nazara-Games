Nazara Technologies has grown from a games portal built by a tech-obsessed college student to India’s only listed games company, a multi-million dollar diversified gaming & sports media platform.
Beginning in 1999 and riding the waves of various dotcom booms & busts, global recessions & recoveries, the explosion in smartphone usage and the digitisation of our everyday lives, Nazara Technologies has survived the tests of time in what was a nascent space for Indian entrepreneurs.
Through it all, we have had one guiding principle: build a self-sustaining company that makes India THE gaming nation of the world.
